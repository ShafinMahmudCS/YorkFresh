Challenge:

How might we improve access to affordable healthy food options on Campus?

Our plan to improve access to affordable healthy food options on Campus:

If a student orders groceries three times from YorkFresh and can prove to help the community or help the environment, they get a $30 voucher to spend on their fourth grocery order.

It can be less if we see the micro point of view, but sixty-two thousand students are studying at York University, even if ten thousand students show up, there will be ten thousand different stories of helping our community or the environment, and not only that, there will be $30000 worth of vouchers provided to students. Besides, these stories will be featured on York Fresh website to inspire others to participate.

We will contact a grocery store, and as we will serve on a large scale, the grocery store will provide free delivery and vouchers because this will ultimately increase their sales, increasing their profit. Moreover, as we will be indirectly helping the environment and the community so potentially, there will be sponsorships and donations, which will be entirely used to cover the expense.
